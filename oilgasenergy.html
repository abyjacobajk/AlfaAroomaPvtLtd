Efficient, safe and environmentally sound

Demands on the oil and gas industry are increasing. Operational reliability, cost containment, safety standards and environmental protection are major considerations. AAE’s wide range of process systems-related solutions not only meet our customers’ exacting process-critical requirements, they comply with the most stringent safety and environmental standards and contribute considerably to reducing operating costs.

Whether delivering complete, large-scale, highly-engineered process refrigeration and gas compression solutions or proven equipment for a specific operating function — including compressor packages, centrifuges, A/C compressors or steam ejector units — AAE's stands ready to meet our customers’ process-critical requirements in the oil & gas space.
