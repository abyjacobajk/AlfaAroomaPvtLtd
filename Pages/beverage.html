Extinguishing the thirst

Beverage market is experiencing dynamic growth worldwide and there is an increasing variety of new products to meet demand, from fruit juices, even with natural bits, to isotonic and health drinks. AAE is a leader in providing the technological and technical expertise to help producers, brewers and wine makers to quench the global thirst for beverages. We develop innovative solutions for manufacturing and preparing juice, syrup, soft drinks, drink concentrates, coffee, tea, soy drinks, spirits, wine and beer. Our expertise extends from complete turnkey projects, engineering, planning and construction to process automation, refrigeration, aseptic and ultra clean filling and packaging. AAE beverage technology is easy to install and maintain, enabling customers to benefit from significantly lower operating costs, while achieving the highest quality production.As well as complying with international quality and hygiene standards, our components and systems are designed to minimize the consumption of natural resources − such as water, energy and raw materials − to reduce the customers’ environmental footprint.
