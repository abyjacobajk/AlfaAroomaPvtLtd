EMISSION CONTROL

Emission control system, in automobiles, means employed to limit the discharge of noxious gases from the internal-combustion engine and other components. The process industry is a global industry and harmful emissions created from different types of process technologies affect many regions worldwide.It is important to realize that the objective of engine optimization is not to minimize the pollutant emissions from the combustion system or maximize the reduction of pollutants in the ATS. Rather the objective is to achieve a target level of emissions from the entire system.
