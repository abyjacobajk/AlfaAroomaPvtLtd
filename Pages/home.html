Home Care

From bulk ingredients to minor additions, from miscible liquids to hard-to-disperse powders, from pasty and wax-based products to flammable and water-like ones, and from clear liquids to nano-emulsions, AAE has the experience and know-how to improve and optimize your home care manufacturing processes..

The processes used in these areas present very specific challenges and require very specific skills. By developing and executing projects using dedicated specialized resources and by dealing with a wide range of equipment and application troubleshooting through our design freedom concept, AAE has developed a unique legacy of expertise in dealing with the significant variability of ingredients handling, mixing techniques, product characteristics, cleaning and hygienic processing.

Powder handling is a key part of many home care processing plants. AAE can design, supply and commission entire plants or provide advanced engineering to handle fragile products and meet all appropriate sanitary requirements.
